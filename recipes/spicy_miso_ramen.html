Spicy Miso Ramen

Description

This spicy miso ramen is a delicious and easy meal that can be made in under 30 minutes! It's packed with flavor and perfect for a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp minced ginger
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup white miso paste
  • 1 tbsp sriracha
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp chili garlic sauce
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup shiitake mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/2 cup sliced red bell pepper
  • 2 cups fresh flour noodle

Steps

  1. Heat a dutch oven and cook protein with some oil
  2. Add garlic, ginger, and green onions and cook until fragrant
  3. Add miso paste, sriracha, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and chili garlic sauce and cook for 1 minute
  4. Add vegetable broth and water and bring to a boil
  5. Add mushrooms, carrots, and bell pepper and cook for 5 minutes
  6. Add noodles and cook for 3 minutes
  7. Top with green onions and serve
