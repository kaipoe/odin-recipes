Turkish Omelette

Description

This versatile egg-based dish is effortless and quick to make, and the things you can serve it with are limitless.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Seasoning
  • Cheese
  • Dill
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a mixing bowl then beat well with a fork or whisk.
  2. Add the milk, flour, and seasoning and beat until smooth.
  3. Heat a little oil in a frying pan and pour in the egg mixture.
  4. When the omelette is almost cooked, sprinkle over the cheese, dill, and parsley.
  5. Flip the omelette over and cook for a further 1-2 minutes.
  6. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve.
