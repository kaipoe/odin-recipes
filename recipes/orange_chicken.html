Orange Chicken

Description

This orange chicken recipe is suitable for anyone's busy schedule. The sweet and tangy sauce covers a crispy air-fried chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Orange Juice
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Rice Vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon Ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Red Pepper Flakes
  • Orange Zest
  • 1 tablespoon Cornstarch

Steps

  1. Orange Sauce: In a medium pot, add orange juice, sugar, vinegar, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and red chili flakes. Heat for 3 minutes.
  2. Orange Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together cornstarch and 1 tablespoon water. Add to the sauce and whisk to combine. Bring to a boil and boil for 1-2 minutes until thickened. Remove from heat and stir in orange zest.
  3. Chicken: In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, cornstarch, salt, and pepper. Add in egg and water and whisk until smooth.
  4. Chicken: Dip chicken pieces into batter and place in a single layer in the air fryer basket. Spray with cooking spray.
  5. Chicken: Air fry at 400 degrees for 10 minutes, flipping halfway through. Repeat with remaining chicken.
  6. Chicken: Toss chicken with orange sauce and serve immediately.
